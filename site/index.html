Methodology

Data comes from the USAJobs API, which I started polling in mid-2025. The dataset contains all 2210 (IT Management) series postings that appeared in the current_jobs snapshot from that point forward. Each posting's Education field and Qualifications Summary were classified using GPT-5.4-mini with structured output, then all non-obvious classifications were verified by GPT-5.4. Code and data are on GitHub.

no_education: No degree required at any grade level. This includes postings where education can substitute for experience — since you can qualify with experience alone, there is no education requirement. It also includes postings where a degree is "preferred" but not required, and postings referencing OPM Alternative A (which allows experience or education).

education_required: A bachelor's degree or higher is explicitly required with no experience-only alternative.

education_required_higher: Degree required only at higher grade levels, not lower ones.
